What is the purpose of a laser cutter for sheet metal?

What are the benefits of using aluminum tubing in manufacturing?

Aluminum tubing is lightweight, highly durable, and provides excellent resistance to corrosion, making it perfect for aerospace, automotive, and architectural applications.

How is aluminum tubing produced?

It is commonly produced using the extrusion process where aluminum billets are heated and forced through a shaped die.

What are the common applications of aluminum tubing?

Frames, structural components, heat exchangers, fluid lines, and more.

Can aluminum tubing be customized?

Yes, aluminum tubing can be custom extruded to precise lengths, shapes, and wall thicknesses depending on project requirements.
